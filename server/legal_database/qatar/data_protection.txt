Article 5: Data Minimization
Collect only necessary data. Retention periods specified.

Article 10: Data Processing
Must have legal basis for processing. Consent must be freely given. Data used only for stated purposes.

Article 12: Individual Rights
Right to access, correct, delete. 30-day response time.

Article 15: Data Security
Appropriate security measures required. Breaches reported within 72 hours. Encryption required for transmission.

Article 18: Data Protection Officer
Required for large-scale processing.

Article 20: Cross-Border Transfers
Personal data transfers require adequate protection. Standard clauses may be used.

Article 25: Penalties
Fines up to QAR 5,000,000 for violations.
