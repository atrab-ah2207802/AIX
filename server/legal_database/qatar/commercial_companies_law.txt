Article 15: Authority to Contract
Company representatives must have proper authority. Board approval required for material contracts.

Article 20: Corporate Capacity
Companies bound by acts within corporate purpose.

Article 25: Ultra Vires Doctrine
Acts beyond corporate capacity may be voidable.

Article 30: Director Duties
Fiduciary duties of care and loyalty. Conflict disclosure required.

Article 45: Related Party Transactions
Contracts with directors/shareholders require disclosure and must be arm's length. Conflict rules apply.

Article 50: Shareholder Approval
Major transactions require shareholder approval.

Article 55: Capital Maintenance
Distributions cannot impair capital.

Article 60: Financial Obligations
Companies must maintain adequate capital. Cannot enter contracts imperiling solvency.

Article 65: Corporate Records
Minutes and resolutions must be maintained.
