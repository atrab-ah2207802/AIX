SECTION 4: Implied Term About Care and Skill - In a contract for the supply of a service where the supplier is acting in the course of a business, there is an implied term that the supplier will carry out the service with reasonable care and skill.

SECTION 5: Implied Term About Time for Performance - Where the time for performance is not fixed by the contract, the supplier will carry out the service within a reasonable time.

SECTION 6: Implied Term About Consideration - Where the consideration for the service is not determined by the contract, the party contracting with the supplier will pay a reasonable charge.

SECTION 13: Right to Supply - In a contract for the supply of a service, there is an implied term that the supplier will supply the service with reasonable care and skill.

SECTION 14: Time for Performance - Where the time for performance is not fixed by the contract, the supplier will perform within a reasonable time.
