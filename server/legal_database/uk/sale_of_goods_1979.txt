SECTION 13: Implied Term About Description - Where there is a contract for the sale of goods by description, there is an implied term that the goods will correspond with the description.

SECTION 14: Implied Terms About Quality or Fitness - Where the seller sells goods in the course of a business, there is an implied term that the goods supplied under the contract are of satisfactory quality.

SECTION 15: Sale by Sample - In a contract for sale by sample, there is an implied term that the bulk will correspond with the sample in quality.
